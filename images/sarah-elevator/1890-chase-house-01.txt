The James Chase House on the bluff in Oregob City, Oregon. The house was
bought by Chases in 1888 or 1889. The picture was taken in 1890. The family
is pictured from L to R. (from left to right on the porch of the Chase house)
Nettie, a young man taking the place of Olney (who had drowned in February of
that year (1890); Dorothy; James Chase and Sade (my grandmother) on the porch,
Ina; and (Sarah) Augusta in front of the porch railing.
